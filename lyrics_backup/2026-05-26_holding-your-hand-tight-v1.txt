[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
I can feel the fire beneath your skin tonight
Your fingers tracing shadows in the neon light
Every breath you take is pulling me inside
Holding your hand tight for the midnight ride

[Verse 1]
We left the city noise a hundred miles behind
Just the steady engine and the quiet of your mind
I see the way you look from the passenger seat
A slow-burn attraction making me complete

[Pre-Chorus]
The radio is playing our favorite old song
You lean a little closer right where you belong
I can taste the longing in the evening air
While I run my fingers through your tangled hair

[Chorus]
When I am holding your hand tight under the velvet sky
I get lost in the magic of your sleepy eyes
It is a soulful rhythm when our bodies align
I just want to make every piece of your heart mine

[Post-Chorus]
Holding your hand tight as the shadows fall
I am answering the whisper of your late-night call

[Verse 2]
The scent of your perfume is lingering on my coat

I just want to stay here and hold you for a while

[Pre-Chorus 2]
